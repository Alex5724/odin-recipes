Panini

Description

Panini is a warm, grilled sandwich made with hearty Italian bread and bold fillings. You layer ingredients like cured meats, cheeses, and veggies between slices of ciabatta or focaccia. The bread is brushed with olive oil or butter, then pressed and grilled until golden and crispy outside, with melted goodness inside.

Ingredients

  • Ciabatta or focaccia bread (sliced)
  • Mozzarella, provolone, or cheddar cheese
  • Cured meats like prosciutto, salami, or ham
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Fresh spinach or arugula
  • Grilled onions or roasted peppers (optional)
  • Basil or pesto for extra flavor
  • Olive oil or butter for brushing the bread

Steps

  1. Slice your ciabatta or focaccia bread in half
  2. Brush the outsides with olive oil or butter
  3. Layer cheese, meats, veggies, and extras inside
  4. Close the sandwich and place it in a panini press or hot skillet
  5. Grill until the bread is crispy and golden, and the cheese is melted
  6. Remove, cut, and serve warm
  7. Enjoy
