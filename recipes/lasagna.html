Description

Lasagna starts with a flavorful ragu, made from minced meat, onion, and tomatoes, slowly cooked with Italian herbs. A creamy béchamel sauce with butter, flour, and milk adds smoothness.

It’s layered in a baking dish, alternating lasagna sheets, sauces, and shredded cheese. Baked until golden, then rested briefly before serving.
