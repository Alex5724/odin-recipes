Lasagna

Description

Lasagna starts with a flavorful ragu, made from minced meat, onion, and tomatoes, slowly cooked with Italian herbs. A creamy béchamel sauce with butter, flour, and milk adds smoothness.

It’s layered in a baking dish, alternating lasagna sheets, sauces, and shredded cheese. Baked until golden, then rested briefly before serving.

Ingredients

  • Minced beef or pork
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Canned tomatoes or tomato passata
  • Olive oil
  • Italian herbs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Grated mozzarella and/or Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the ragu sauce with meat, onion, garlic, tomatoes, and spices.
  2. Boil the lasagna sheets if needed.
  3. Layer sheets, sauce, and grated cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Bake until golden and bubbly.
  5. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
