Carbonara

Description

Mix the eggs with cheese and pepper, then toss with hot pasta off the heat. The warmth cooks the sauce gently becoming creamy and rich, no cream needed!

Ingredientes

  • Pasta
  • Guanciale
  • Egg yolk
  • Pecorino romano
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Grate Pecorino Romano and mix with egg yolks and black pepper
  2. Cut guanciale into strips and cook until crispy.
  3. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
  4. Save a bit of pasta water before draining.
  5. Combine hot pasta with guanciale (off the heat).
  6. Quickly stir in egg mixture, adding pasta water as needed for creaminess.
  7. Serve immediately with extra Pecorino and black pepper.
